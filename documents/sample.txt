Product Line Overview
Acme Widget Corporation manufactures three product lines: the WidgetPro series,
the WidgetLite series, and the WidgetMax industrial series.

WidgetPro Series
The WidgetPro 3000 was released in Q2 2024. It features a titanium housing,
40-hour battery life, and is compatible with the Acme Connect mobile app version
4.2 and above. The recommended retail price is $349 USD. The WidgetPro 3000 is
available in Midnight Blue, Arctic White, and Graphite.

WidgetLite Series
The WidgetLite is our entry-level offering, priced at $129 USD. It has a
plastic housing, 18-hour battery life, and connects via Bluetooth 5.1.
It is not compatible with the Acme Connect app but works with standard
Bluetooth audio profiles.

WidgetMax Industrial Series
The WidgetMax is designed for factory floor use. It features an IP68 waterproof
rating, a 72-hour battery, and integrates with Acme's enterprise fleet management
software. Minimum order quantity is 50 units. Contact enterprise-sales@acme.example
for pricing.

Support Policy
All Acme products come with a 2-year warranty. Warranty claims must be submitted
through the Acme support portal at support.acme.example within 30 days of the defect
being discovered. Proof of purchase is required. The warranty does not cover
physical damage or water damage for non-WidgetMax models.

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Can I use the WidgetPro 3000 underwater?
A: No. The WidgetPro 3000 is splash-resistant (IPX4) but not waterproof. Only the
WidgetMax carries a full IP68 waterproof rating.

Q: What is the return policy?
A: Products may be returned within 14 days of purchase for a full refund, provided
they are in original condition with all packaging. After 14 days, warranty service
applies for defects.
